## Current highlights

- Dynamic earthquake details with catalog provenance, recording availability, waveform access, and community reports.
- Map basemaps and operational overlays for stations, earthquakes, faults, plate boundaries, and the Philippine Area of Responsibility.
- Sender health snapshots, token/update/watchdog state, restricted alerts, and automatic update recovery.
- Reverse SSH-over-WebSocket remote access through ``wstunnel`` with enrollment and operator controls.
- Deployment nginx hardening, restricted service routing, crawler controls, and SeisComP inventory-import workflows.
